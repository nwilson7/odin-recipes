Roast Chicken Recipe

Description

Use just one pan to make a complete roasted chicken dinner with roasted carrots, potatoes and a creamy herb gravy.

Ingredients

  • Red Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Salt and pepper
  • Whole Chicken
  • Olive Oil
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Roast chicken in oven
  3. Make gravy
