Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2x Can of tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Many Cheeses
  • Egg
  • Spices and Seasonings

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
